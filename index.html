HOW TO PLAY

🏁 START — You have $100,000,000 to build a Bakken empire.

🗺️ MAP — Click the satellite map to place well pads. Drag to pan, scroll to zoom.

🏗️ PADS — Build multi-well pads with 1-6 wells each. Shared facilities save money.

⛏️ DRILL — Each well goes through Permitting → Drilling → Completing → Flowback → Producing.

🛢️ PRODUCE — Watch animated pumpjacks and rigs on the map as wells produce.

🔧 LIFT — Choose Rod Pump, ESP, or Gas Lift when natural flow declines.

💰 REVENUE — Based on simulated WTI & gas prices with realistic decline curves.

⚠️ EVENTS — Blowouts, freeze-offs, stuck pipe, and more. Stay sharp.

💀 GAME OVER — If cash drops below -$10M, you're bankrupt.
